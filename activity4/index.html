The Elder Paradox

While the Elder Wand is famous for being the most powerful weapon in wizarding history, traditional wandlore actually warns against using its materials. Famous wandmaker Garrick Ollivander noted that elder wood is the rarest wood of all to be matched with a wizard, and that elder-wood wands almost never choose a mediocre magical user. Furthermore, the old wizarding rhyme, "Wand of elder, never prosper," was born because owners of elder wands historically drew a lot of unwanted, violent attention to themselves!
